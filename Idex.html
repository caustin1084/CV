Personal Statement

Hello, my name is Chanelle Austin, and I am passionate about my community. I believe it is my life’s mission to gain knowledge of the United States Food System and how our daily dietary habits are affecting our health and wellness. I enjoy sharing my knowledge with others and challenging myself to always learn more. I began my journey for knowledge of food by attending Johnson and Wales University, where I completed a Bachelor of Science in Food Service Management with a concentration in Sustainability. Next, I enrolled in a year-long farmer apprenticeship. Which introduced husbandry, sustainable animal management, and growing and harvesting certified organic vegetables. In my future studies, I will continue my education and include knowledge of herbs and dietary supplements. In addition to my degree and certification, I also have more than five years of experience in hospitality. This experience has empowered me to take on leadership roles, provide exceptional customer service, and solve difficult concerns of customers, co-workers, and businesses alike.
